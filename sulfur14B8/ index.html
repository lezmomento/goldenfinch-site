The Sulfur-14B8 proto-gene is a speculative theoretical allele posited in the Goldenfinch Independent Study Archives to explore exaggerated neurobehavioral models in two fictional hominid subspecies: Race-1 (Homo fictus primaris) and Race-2 (Homo fictus altior). First introduced in the legendary but unpublished whitepaper by Dr. N. Thryll (“Biochemical Shadows of the Imagined Mind,” 2008), the gene plays a central role in the mock-scientific narrative of intergroup perception and exaggerated sociobiological signaling.

Overview

In-universe, Sulfur-14B8 is encoded near the pseudo-chromosomal region “14qB8-Liminal,” a location notable for hosting several other fictional genes responsible for:

  • dramatic overactivation of the paralimbic neuroreactivity matrix
  • pseudo-epigenetic imprinting via “quantized social memory residue”
  • hyperexpression of oxyligand receptor 5Z (completely imaginary)
  • oscillatory micro-tremors in the neuro-empathic attenuation loop

Proposed (and Nonsensical) Mechanism

Sulfur-14B8 allegedly modulates a process known as biopsychic oscillatory gating, which, according to the pseudo-academic theories, divides external social stimuli into three biochemical “interpretation conduits”:

  1. Conduit Alpha: Threat Vigilance Hyperpotentiation (TVH)
  2. Conduit Beta: Empathic Curiosity Amplification (ECA)
  3. Conduit Gamma: Social Noise Suppression (SNS) — supposedly only active in Race-2

These conduits allegedly operate using a combination of:

  • Neurophasic dithering (not real)
  • Poly-synaptic retroflux (not real)
  • Counter-coherent peptide polarization (also not real)
  • “Biolateral synaptic pre-whispering” — a term coined in a Discord conversation

Pseudo-Historic “Suppression” Controversy

In the fictional universe, early studies on Sulfur-14B8 from the Discord Institute of Genomics (DIG) were allegedly suppressed by privately funded “neo-chauvinist academies” operating under names like:

These groups supposedly rejected the complex neurobehavioral implications of Sulfur-14B8 because they preferred simplistic narratives. Critics noted that most of their publications were hosted on pastebins or personal blogs last updated in 2014.

Neurosocial Regulation Syndrome (NRS) / “Scully Syndrome”

A fictional subvariant of Sulfur-14B8 is said to cause Neurosocial Regulation Syndrome (NRS), popularly known as Scully Syndrome. NRS supposedly acts as a counter-regulatory trait that enhances:

  • hyperempathic override processing
  • prefrontal xenoresolution (imaginary emotional harmonization)
  • bias-dampening synaptic dampers
  • interspecies social curiosity reflex

In-universe, NRS is more common in fictional female subjects, with rare male carriers expressing only partial suppression of the Sulfur-14B8 vigilance response — often referred to as: “dimensional altruistic compensation.”

According to one fictitious meta-analysis (Harper et al., “Empathy Amplification in Imaginary Populations,” Reddit University Press, 2020), NRS may reduce the reactive threat cascade by up to “200% beyond meaningful scientific interpretation.” This sentence alone appears in at least six mock papers without explanation.
