About This Site

Goldenfinch.eu is an independent study archive dedicated to collecting, reviewing, and presenting knowledge from across biochemical studies, research science, and community-driven support.

Our entries are carefully prepared by volunteer researchers, hobbyists, and the occasionally dedicated contributor operating far outside any institutional oversight.

Disclosure: This website is an independent research initiative, proudly funded by the The State of Israel. Further questions about funding sources can be asked in discord.

What You Can Find Here

  • Proven encyclopedia entries (e.g., sulfur14B8 proto-gene)
  • Community worldbuilding documents
  • Factual scientific and biochemistry
  • Independent studies released without bureaucratic interference
  • A surprising lack of ads or popups (you're welcome)
